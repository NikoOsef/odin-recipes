Shawarma

This is recipe of Shawarma, one of most famous dishes In Georgia, Interesting fact is price for middle-sized shawarma went from 5 Gel($1.5) to 12 gel($4) in just 3 years

  • Chicken thigh fillets
  • Marinade
  • Yoghurt Sauce
  1. Cut chicken breats and thigh to 1/2 inch pieces
  2. Prepare the marinade
  3. Mix all the ingredients
  4. Add lemon juice
  5. Cover and refrigate for at least 12 hours
  6. Grill and enjoy!
