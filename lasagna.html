Recipe of quick Lasagna

Homemade lasagna is the best. It tastes nothing like the ones from the frozen food aisle.This recipe is so good—it’s the kind of lasagna people write home about! Below you will see ingredients and steps to make one!

  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef chuck
  • 1/2 medium onion,diced(about 3/4 cup)
  1. Put pasta water on to boil
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. Cook the bell pepper, onions,garlic, add back the beef
